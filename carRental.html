Car Rental Management System

Car Rental Management System is a web-based car reservation system, capable of handling all business aspects a Car Hire Broker can imagine. The system is individually configurable in accordance with the wishes and business requirements of Car Rental. It meets the specifications of Industry Systems Standards. Compliance with the standards allows for its flexible interconnection to any third party systems.

Complete software for managing your car rental business. It is a system designed specifically for medium and small car rentals. It is packed with features such as a one screen rental process, reservation sales, fleet utilization, rate management and accounting interfaces. Main benefits it can provide are listed below:

  • Multi-Lingual
  • Self-Service
  • Marketing & Advertising
  • CSV export
  • Cost Effectiveness
  • Yield Management
  • Payment reports
  • Multimarket
  • Maintenance Module
  • User Friendly
  • Flexibility
  • Compatibility
  • Problem Solving
  • Keeping Pace With Technology
  • Quality Assurance
