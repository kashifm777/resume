Budget Manager

Budget Manager helps manage personal budgets. After adding your budgets, simply record your day-to-day transactions. You can then view how close your sending is to your budget. It helps you keep track of your spending, and keep your data as your own. The application requires very few permissions, and never attempts to access the Internet. There is an option to backup your cards to local storage. From there you can send the backup somewhere safe.
